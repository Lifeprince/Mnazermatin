FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ethical hacking?

Ethical hacking is the practice of testing and protecting information systems with the owner's consent to identify potential vulnerabilities and improve security.

How can I improve my cyber security?

You can enhance your cyber security through measures such as regular updates, strong passwords, employee training, and implementing security protocols.

What should I do in case of a security breach?

In the event of a security breach, it is crucial to contain the incident, preserve evidence, and engage a cyber security professional for incident response and forensic investigation.

What is the importance of security awareness training?

Security awareness training helps individuals recognize and respond to cyber threats, thereby reducing the likelihood of successful attacks and protecting sensitive information.

Why is network security essential for businesses?

Network security safeguards sensitive data, prevents unauthorized access, and ensures the integrity and availability of business-critical assets and operations.
